The determinant of a [[Matrix]] has the following properties for a matrix $A$ of order $n$ and a constant $c$:

1. $|AB| = |A||B|$
2. $|A^{-1}|=\frac{1}{|A|}$
3. $|cA|=c^{n}|A|$
4. Row operations across matrices result in identical determinants
5. $|A^{T}| = |A|$
